About Us

KEN (Knowledge Enlightenment Network) Academy was established to create knowledge in graduates and preparing them to job ready in government and private sectors. KEN has successfully implemented a variety of programs for students across different colleges, including Engineering, Degree and polytechnic institutions with 99% success rate in Placements. KEN also works with already graduated individuals to take their careers to the next level.

Mission

KEN is committed to assure learning experiences that will assist our students to use their greatest potential to achieve success in all the career oriented campus and competitive examinations.

KEN recognises that each student is an individual, that all students are intelligent, that all students need stimulus to work hard and every student needs success. Therefore, KEN respect the individual needs of students, creates a learning environment and develops methodology that focuses on learning styles.

Vision

KEN will always strive to devise the teaching techniques that maximizes individual potential and ensure that students of all ability levels are well equipped to meet the challenges of competitive environment.

KEN Pledges to contribute to the society by enhancing knowledge and skills of engineers through all possible innovative techniques.
